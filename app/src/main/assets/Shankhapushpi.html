SHANKHPUSHPI

When we need mental peace and sleep, we may call upon shankhapushi to help restore clear quality of the mind and relaxation. Its flowers resemble the shankha or conch shell, thus the name. This herb is an outstanding rejuvenative tonic for the mind and nerve tissue. It is said to have profound mystical properties, with an affinity for the heart, throat, third eye, and crown chakras.

The herb is especially effective for mental peace and tranquility. Often taken with or prepared in ghee, it promotes serenity without dulling the mind. As it is balancing, its effect can be both uplifting and calming. Students traditionally use it to support a calm mind during exams

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • For support of focus and attention, it combines well with brahmi and licorice
  • For support of healthy sleep patterns, use shankhapushpi at bedtime (traditional use allows for up to 6 grams)
  • ghee, cooked three times with shankhapushpi juice and milk, makes even the dullest mind sharp.
