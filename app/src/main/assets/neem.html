NEEM

Neem is known for its ability to heal cuts and wounds. It also has antiseptic properties, making it an ideal ingredient for a dog bite home remedy

INGREDIENTS :

  • To use neem essential oil, add 10 drops to 2 tablespoons of aloe vera or coconut oil.
  • You can also crush neem leaves and mix it with aloe vera.
  • Apply the paste and leave overnight.
  • While you can use any carrier oil, aloe vera will have a cooling effect on the wound.
