BAEL LEAF POWDER

Bael leaves and the bael fruit which is also known as wood apple is found to be an excellent ingredient that will help in soothing stomach muscles and help in reducing loose motion problems.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Take 25 grams of dried bael leaf and fruit powder and mix it with 2 tablespoons of honey.
  • Take this mixture three to four times a day and repeat it every day until you feel that you are free from loose motion.
