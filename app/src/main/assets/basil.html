TULASI

A strong-scented herb which is used for natural headache treatment, it has many analgesic benefits. The oil works as a muscle relaxant and helps get rid of headaches caused by tension and tight muscles.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • put 3 or 4 fresh basil leaves in a cup of boiling water and let it simmer.
  • Add a little honey and sip the tea slowly.
  • You can also chew some fresh basil leaves, or inhale the steam after boiling basil in a pot of water.
