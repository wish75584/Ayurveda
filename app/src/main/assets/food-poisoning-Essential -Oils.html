ESSENTIAL OILS

Oregano Oil

Oregano essential oil can work wonders in treating food poisoning. It contains compounds like carvacrol and thymol that impart excellent antimicrobial properties to it and help eliminate the pathogens responsible for food poisoning (4).

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Add a drop of oregano oil to a glass of water and mix well.
  • Consume this solution.
  • You must drink this 1 to 2 times daily until you see an improvement in the symptoms.

Thyme Oil

Thyme oil contains compounds like thymol, carvacrol, and linalool that have antimicrobial and antiparasitic properties (5). Hence, it can be effective against various foodborne illnesses.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Add a drop of thyme oil to a glass of water.
  • Mix well and consume this..
  • Drink this solution 1 to 2 times daily.
