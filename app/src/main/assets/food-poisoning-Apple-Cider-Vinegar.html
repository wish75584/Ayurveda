APPLE VINEGAR

The antibacterial properties of apple cider vinegar are very effective against foodborne pathogenic bacteria like Escherichia coli (1), (2). ACV is also a rich source of minerals and enzymes that can help in stabilizing your body after a bout of food poisoning (3).

INGREDIENTS :

  • Add one to two tablespoons of apple cider vinegar to a glass of water.
  • Mix well and consume immediately.
  • You must drink this 2 to 3 times daily

INSTUCTIONS :

  • Combine all ingredients in a mug of your choice.
  • Allow the ingredients to simmer in 2 cups of water for five minutes.
  • Drink three times daily.
