What Is Food Poisoning?

When you ingest contaminated, expired, or toxic food, it often leads to food poisoning, also known as foodborne illness. It is usually characterized by nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. The other symptoms are discussed below.

Signs And Symptoms Of Food Poisoning

  • Abdominal pain or cramps
  • Loss of appetite
  • Fever
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Headaches
