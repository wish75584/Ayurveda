BASIL

Basil with Honey

Basil is another herb known to kill foodborne pathogens with its excellent antimicrobial properties (14). It can also calm your stomach and reduce the symptoms associated with food poisoning.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Crush some basil leaves and extract the juice.
  • Mix a teaspoon of honey with a teaspoon of basil extract and consume immediately.
  • Alternatively, you can also add a drop of basil oil to a cup of water and consume it.

Basil With Cardamom

The antimicrobial properties of tulsi help eliminate the microbes that cause food poisoning. The combination of tulsi and cardamom works wonders in alleviating vomiting and nausea (15).

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Chew on the basil leaves along with cardamom.
  • Alternatively, you can mix basil extract with cardamom powder and consume a teaspoon of it.
