PLUMS

Plums are a great source of antioxidants and are known to be very hydrating. These antioxidants lend anti-inflammatory properties that help in soothing internal inflammation that may be caused due to a heat stroke.

STEPS TO FOLLOW:

  • Soak some plums in water till they are soft.
  • Mash them in the water and strain it.

This drink should be consumed as a remedy for a heat stroke. It is known to calm your body.
