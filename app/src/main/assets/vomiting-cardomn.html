CARDOMN

A simple yet effective remedy that Ayurveda recommends, irrespective of your dosha type:

STEPS TO FOLLOW:

  • chew 1 or two cardamom seeds, the change of taste can also help soothe the vomiting sensation
  • Black cardamom subdues kapha, pitta, and vata, while green cardamom stimulates pitta and soothes vata and kapha.
