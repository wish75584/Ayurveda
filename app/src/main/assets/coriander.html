CORIANDER

Coriander or cilantro is a wonderful source of dietary fiber, manganese, iron and magnesium as well. In addition, coriander leaves are rich in Vitamin C, Vitamin K and protein. They also contain small amounts of calcium, phosphorous, potassium, thiamin, niacin and carotene.

Coriander Tea

The seeds of coriander contain phytonutrients and vitamins to boost an individual’s immune system. Despite this, its powerful anti-microbial components and essential oils make it one of the best home remedies for fever. To prepare a healthy coriander tea, follow the steps written below:

INGREDIENTS :

  • Add one teaspoon of coriander seeds in a glass containing water.
  • Boil the mixture for 10 minutes.
  • Once it is warm, strain and drink it.
  • If you don’t like its taste, we suggest you add some palm jaggery to it.
