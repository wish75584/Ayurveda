TRIPHALA

The best ayurvedic remedy for constipation, triphala is both a tonic and a laxative. Classically, triphala is taken upon rising and just before bed.

STEPS TO FOLLOW:

  • Steep ½ to 1 teaspoon in a cup of hot water 5 to 10 minutes.
  • If you can’t stand the taste, take 2 capsules with warm water.
