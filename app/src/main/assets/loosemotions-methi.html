METHI DANA

Fenugreek has antibacterial and antifungal properties and is also an excellent home remedy to fight loose stools.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Take one to two teaspoons of dried fenugreek seeds and powder it in a blender to reduce it into fine powder.
  • Now mix this powder in a glass of water and take it every day in the morning on an empty stomach for two to three days to completely get relief from loose motions.
