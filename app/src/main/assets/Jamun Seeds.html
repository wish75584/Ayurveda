JAMUN SEEDS

The seed powder of Eugenia jambolana (1 tsp) twice a day along with lukewarm water is good in curing of it. The chewing of Jamun leaves prevents the conversion of starch into sugar.

INGREDIENTS :

  • Crush the bitter melon into a grinder after separating its seeds.
  • Little bit of water may also be added. Sieve this to have juice
  • Cut the bitter melon into small and thin pieces
  • Put small quantity of mustard oil and salt as required into the fry pan.
  • Heat the ingredients along with onion and green chili for about 10-15 minutes.
