GINGER

Adrak or ginger can be consumed fresh or dry (powder) to get relief from arthritis. Steep a couple of fresh slices of ginger to make an herbal tea for joint pain. Make this ginger tea with other joint-soothing ingredients like turmeric and cinnamon, and sweeten with honey to relax your painful knees and elbows. Add a few drops of ginger oil to two tablespoons of warm mustard oil. Massage the affected joints with this oil to reduce swelling and pain. One can also make a warm compress by boiling some ginger and tea leaves together. Take off the flame and let it cool slightly. Dip a cloth into the tea and apply the cloth as a compress on the affected area.
