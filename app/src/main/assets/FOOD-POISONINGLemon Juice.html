LEMON JUICE

Lemon juice is a rich source of antioxidants that improve the overall healing of your body. It exhibits exceptional bactericidal activities that can help combat the bacterial pathogens responsible for causing food poisoning (12), (13). Lemon juice also aids digestion and helps you recover faster.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Extract the juice from half a lemon and mix it with a glass of water.
  • Add some honey for flavor and consume.
  • You can drink lemon juice 2 to 3 times daily.
