Stay Warm.

Don’t think that it’s impossible for you to catch a cold from being cold. Sleeping in an overly air-conditioned room or going without a coat for too long in the winter is enough to make you sick. Wear an undershirt that covers your chest (a main site of kapha, which is out of balance during a cold) and layer with a warm wool sweater. Use a hairdryer after showering and always cover your head and body when going outside.
