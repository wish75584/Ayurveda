BLACK PAPER

During the absence of hydrochloric acid in our stomach, pepper can be very effective to treat the problem. Black pepper can increase the flow of gastric juices and thus can help in digestion. Black pepper made with grated molasses can be taken along with buttermilk during indigestion. You can also prepare a mixture of powdered black pepper, powder ginger, coriander seeds and mint leaves, and one spoon of this mixture can be eaten every day.
