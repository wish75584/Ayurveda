Coconut Oil

Supplementing your body with coconut oil can help in strengthening your bones and prevent bone loss associated with osteoporosis (9). The anti-inflammatory action of coconut oil can provide relief from pain and inflammation .

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Consume a tablespoon of cold pressed coconut oil via your daily diet.
  • You can also use coconut oil topically on the affected area.
  • Consume coconut oil regularly to get the desired results.
