Bottle Gourd Juice

Bottle gourd juice is an excellent drink that must be given to people suffering from loose motions as it will help in retaining the water content in the body and prevent the body from getting dehydrated.

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Take a fresh bottle gourd, peel the skin and then cut it into small pieces.
  • Squeeze out the juice from the mash mixture and consume it two times a day to restore the water level of the body and to not get dehydrated when suffering from loose motion.
