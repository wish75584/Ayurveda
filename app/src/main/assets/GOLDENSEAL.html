GOLDENSEAL

For centuries, goldenseal has been used as a natural antiseptic and astringent. Its anti-inflammatory powers can even treat allergies and digestive inflammation. So if you need to treat a dog bite, try goldenseal.

INGREDIENTS :

  • The most popular use of goldenseal is to consume the extract.
  • Just a few drops will do.
  • However, you can also add a goldenseal extract directly to the wound.
  • Cover it with a dressing and change twice a day until it heals.
