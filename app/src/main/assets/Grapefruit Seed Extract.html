GRAPE FRUITE EXTRACT

Grapefruit seed extract is derived from whole grape seeds. It contains polyphenols called oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs) that possess antimicrobial and antioxidant properties. These properties fight the pathogens responsible for food poisoning and also help you recover faster (11).

STEPS TO FOLLOW :

  • Add a few drops of grapefruit seed extract to a glass of water and mix well.
  • Consume this solution daily.
