MANUKA HONEY

Pure manuka honey has powerful anti-inflammatory properties. It’s often used for skin care because it can heal wounds prevent bacterial infections. Because of this, manuka honey is one of the best natural remedies for dog bites.

INGREDIENTS :

  • All you need is 3 teaspoons of manuka honey.
  • You may need more or less depending on the size of the wound.
  • After washing and drying the dog bite, apply the honey.
  • Leave it on for several hours.
  • If you’d like, wrap the wound with gauze. This will protect your clothing if you want to wear it overnight
